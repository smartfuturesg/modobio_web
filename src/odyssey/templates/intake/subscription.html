Congratulations! You are ready to take your next steps in developing and following a customized, proactive, and natural response to your personal health issues and goals. Modo Bio works with you, to create a highly personal, continuously assessed health solution, supported by data analysis.

You have already completed your initial consultation. Now, together with your Modo Bio Team Members, you have a highly personalized and sustainable plan for optimization of your well-being, performance, and healthcare. This Subscription Agreement puts the plan into action. Together, you and Modo Bio will help you maximize your health and wellness.

Subscription plan

The Modo Bio Subscription plan provides access to the following services each month:

  • Eight 60-minute training sessions (twice per week) with Parker Condit.
  • One 30-minute consultation with Dr. Michael Kay on your physical movement and activity.
  • One 30-minute consultation on your medical and nutritional progress, for which we will refer you to Dr. Adam Douglas, whose practice is conveniently located adjacent to our offices.
  • Wearable device bio-data monitoring and analysis.

A Modo Bio subscription costs $1,250 per month, for a 3-month commitment. After 3 months, the subscription will automatically renew for another 3-month period.

Individual services

Individual services may be purchased outside of, or in addition to, the subscription plan.

  • Appointment with Dr. Michael Kay or Dr. Adam Douglas: $400 for a 1 hour consultation.
  • Training session with Parker Condit: $120 for a 1 hour session.
  • Wearable device bio-data monitoring: $30 per month.
  • Customized nutritional supplements: $10 per bottle.
  • Nutrition or hormone supplements: $5 per dosage.

Notes

  • Consultations may take place in person or by video, depending on scheduling.
  • Regular participation in all sessions and consultations is required for best results with the Modo Bio program. Sessions and consultations are scheduled on a monthly basis, and unused sessions and consultations do not roll over or carry forward.
  • The Modo Bio program requires you to obtain an Oura Ring device to track bio-data. We have found the Oura Ring superior for tracking relevant information, although we may add other wearables to the program in the future.
  • You may purchase an Oura Ring directly from Oura, or from Modo Bio.
  • You must give Modo Bio access to your wearable device data, which may require you to execute a consent or enter a consent on a website to release your bio-data to us.
  • Bio-data analysis may require up to 9 months or more of data gathering to provide useful information.
  • Bio-data analysis may be performed by a Modo Bio Team Member, or by third-party contractors.
