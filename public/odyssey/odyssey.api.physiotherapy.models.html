Describes pain areas in the client’s body.

The client or staff member indicates pain areas by drawing on the silhouette of a human (front and back) and circling the pain areas. The drawings are translated to bezier curves in canvas coordinates, which are stored as a JSON dictionary.

Mobility assessment table a.k.a the chessboard.

This table stores the repeated mobility assessment measurements. The measurements are indexed by user_id and assessment timestamp. All values in this table, with the exception of isa_dynamic, are angles in degrees.
