Welcome to Modo Bio, and to your first step in developing a customized, proactive, and natural pathway to your personal health and wellness goals. Modo Bio works with you, to create a highly personal and continuously assessed health and wellness solution, supported by data analysis.

Your initial consultation is your first step toward a highly personalized and sustainable optimization of your well-being, performance, and healthcare. A complete initial consultation costs $700 (plus laboratory costs), and consists of two 1-hour modules:

  • The Evaluation Module has 3 components:
    1. Medical and nutritional evaluation with Dr. Adam Douglas, in his independent practice
    2. Physical movement evaluation with Dr. Michael Kay
    3. Introduction to the Training Program with Parker Condit
    Modo Bio will draw blood and potentially other samples for laboratory testing, and may include physical testing for the Training Program.
  • The Consultation Module will be scheduled after return and our evaluation of the samples and lab tests. Modo Bio personnel will review your results and establish a data-optimized action plan to optimize your health and wellness.

You may elect to purchase only the Evaluation Module, in which case the cost is $350 and no blood work or samples will be drawn or evaluated. The Consultation Module is available only with the Evaluation Module as a prerequisite.
