class odyssey.api.lookup.models.LookupTerritoriesofOperation(**kwargs)¶

Bases: sqlalchemy.orm.decl_api.Model

Territories of operaion are organized by country and then sub-territory, where a sub_territory is the highest level of governing region that can have laws which will impact our business practices.

In the UNited States, the su_territory will be at the state level.

Staff members are required to specify which territories they can operate in.
