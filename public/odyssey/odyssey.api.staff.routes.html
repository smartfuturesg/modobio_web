class odyssey.api.staff.routes.StaffCloseAccountEndpoint(*args, **kwargs)

Bases: odyssey.utils.base.resources.BaseResource

Close staff member account.

post(user_id)

Close staff portion of an account.

Closing an account means that the account will be deleted after a number of days (initially 30 days). If the staff member logs in after closing, but before deletion, the account is open again and will not be deleted.

The staff member is logged out immediately after closing the account.
